Our World

A strong foundation and traditional values is a promise of Sureka as a business group. The planning outlook is such that it spans generations ahead, instead of just a few years.

The name Sureka, is an ode to our family name. As descendants of traders, businessmen and merchants who emigrated from deserts to share their knowledge and prosperity with the rest of the country, we are determined to carry on the tradition.

As our group completes its 50th year, our pride and joy has no bounds knowing that not only have we built a successful industry with over 2000 employees, a distribution network of 6000 dealers, 56 offices and operations spanning 3 continents, but also landmark housing and buildings which have stood the test of time. We look forward to creating sustainable social development and safeguarding our environment to build a better world for generations to come.
